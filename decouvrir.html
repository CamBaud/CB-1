Our Project


We have developed our project during a Connected Health Hackathon.
It resulted in a smart bracelet designed for residents which targets assisted living facilities.

The bracelet has sensors to get real time data like :

  • Heart frequency
  • Oxymetry/saturation (blood oxygen levels)
  • Temperature

The aim of this device is to analyze the trends that might occur weeks after weeks
to optimize the medical care given to the residents.

If one of the different data peaks (for instance if blood pressure skyrockets),
an alert is automatically sent onto the nurse's board control and their bippers.

An accelerometer is also dispayed on the bracelet in order to get
the real time information if a resident falls.













Our Product


The product has been designed to fit the expectations of the customers.

It doesn't look like a connected bracelet because it would be
too intrusive for them. The design is based on a watch
in order to have a more familiar products for the residents.

Its Added Value


To have an additive way to prevent residents falls and to rescue
them in case of emergency.

The other purpose of the project is to better the everyday life of nurses
which are generally overwhelmed (40 residents per nurse).
